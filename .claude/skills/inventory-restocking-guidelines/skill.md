# Skill: Inventory Restocking Guidelines

## Trigger
- When evaluating whether to restock a product in inventory
- When reviewing product performance for inventory decisions

## Steps
1. Assess how the product is performing in the market
2. Analyze what type of revenue the product is generating
3. Apply the 20% profit guideline to determine if restocking is justified
4. Make the inventory decision based on the profit threshold analysis

## Output
- A restocking decision that maintains profitability standards
- Inventory levels that align with the company's 20% profit guideline
